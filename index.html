About

Praveen kumar koppolu

Praveen Kumar Koppolu is a Indian playback singer and celebrated singing performer. Known for his unique vocal style and deep, melodious voice, he has won hearts with his exceptional renditions, especially in Telugu songs. His performances are recognized for their expressive musicality and artistry, making him a prominent figure in the world of Indian music.

Through his extraordinary talent on stage, he captivates the hearts of music lovers both in India and abroad. His performances are filled with mesmerizing artistry, leaving audiences spellbound and deeply moved by his music.

Not only with songs but also by showcasing his mark in traditional epic poetry, he is making a significant impact in the world of music. His mastery in these classical forms sets him apart, establishing him as a respected figure in the music realm.

With his singing talent, he has earned the admiration of legendary singer SP Balasubrahmanyam.

He has worked alongside Great music directors like MM Keeravani, Mani Sharma, Raj–Koti, SS Thaman, and Sai Kartheek.

Apart from legendary singers like P. Susheela, S. Janaki, Jamuna Rani, K. S. Chithra, SP Sailaja, Kalpana, Sunitha, MM Sreelekha, Mano, Ramakrishna, and G. Anand, he has also collaborated with many popular contemporary singers in the industry.

Singing not only in Telugu but also in Hindi, Tamil, and Kannada, he captivates the hearts of audiences across languages. With 18 years of experience as a singer, he does not just sing beautifully; his performances, combined with impressive dance moves, win over the hearts of the spectators.

Praveen Kumar Koppolu has a remarkable record of performing an 8-hour-long stage program continuously. This impressive feat showcases his stamina, dedication, and passion for music, making him stand out as a talented playback singer and performer. Such extended performances highlight his ability to engage the audience and maintain high energy levels throughout the event.

Apart from cinema songs and stage performances, Praveen Kumar Koppolu has gained immense recognition for his work in independent albums. He composes and sings in these projects, showcasing his versatility and creativity as an artist. His contributions to independent music have earned him a remarkable reputation, setting him apart in the industry.

Praveen Kumar Koppolu has had the honor of releasing his independent album under the guidance of Oscar-winning lyricist Chandrabose. This collaboration highlights the significance of his work and showcases the respect he has earned in the music industry. The album reflects his artistic vision and has contributed to his growing recognition as a talented independent artist.
