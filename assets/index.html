About

Praveen kumar koppolu

Above 15 years of rich experience in Music for Movie Playback singing,Reality music shows, Talent shows and Music Albums across various countries Equipotent in Singing Telugu, Tamil, Malayalam, Hindi and English Songs Proven Extraordinary ability to render music for a continuous 8 hours on stage

Mastered the art of singing Film music, Folk music, Light music, Devotional songs Classical music, Melodies and other forms of songs in multiple Genres

Performed many musical programmes with famous play back singers S.P.Balasubrahmanyam, S.P.Sailaja, Chitra,Mano, Sunitha,Kalpana

Won praise from many popular Singers,Lyricists and Music Directors Karthik, AnuradhaSriram, G.Anand, Jamuna Rani, Chandrabose,Unnikrishanan,

Disciple of Master D.V. SureshraoGaru in Carnatic Classical music

Performed in USA ,AUSTRALIA , KUWAIT ,NEWZEALAND , BURMA , SINGAPORE , UK (UNITED KINGDOM)
